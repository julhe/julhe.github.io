Project stuff

For the professional work, look at my LinkedIn page.

Lucid Trips (Steam) - Lucid Trips Team (2015+)

A abstract VR-Game with unique locomotion and artworks to explore. I’ve done art-tools, lot’s of custom shaders, effects such as the player trails or permanent footsteps and parts of the sound design.

with Nico Uthe, Sara Lisa Vogl and Peter van der Straat

Tower Tag - VR-Nerds GmbH (2018)

While working at VR-Nerds, I’ve done shader + art + framework that would display damage on destructable walls. Also I helped moving the Projects onto the new Unity Lightweigth Renderpipeline.

Storm (ModDB) (2010 - 2012)

You survied a plane crash and are lost behind enemy lines. Can you survie with only a knife and a proxmity detector? Modification for Crysis.

With CryMod-Community content by Xzero (Shader Package), CoD511 (Testing), Yaxxo (Knife Animation), nonamed817(Knife Model) and CoD511(Knife Rig).

The infinite balloon ride - University Assigment (2016)

You controll a balloon over a infinte, procedual landscape. Made in Unity. I’ve made a framework that generates terrain on-the-fly with the GPU.

ThatSheepGame - Practice (2016+)

Controll a shepard dog to bring all sheep back into thier shed. All assets are handmade. Sheep are using a special flocking algorithmn. This is more of an hobby project to figure out the capabilitys of mobile hardware.

Get Old or Die Trying - University Assigment (2017)
